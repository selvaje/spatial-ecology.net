#### Cost of living in Matera

The average cost of living in Matera is low compared to the rest of Italy, e.g. a meal in a regular restaurant costs about 15-20 Euro. You can find detailed information [here](http://www.numbeo.com/cost-of-living/city_result.jsp?country=Italy&city=Matera).

### Travel Information

The easiest way to reach Matera is to fly to Bari Karol Wojtyla Airport [BRI](http://www.aeroportidipuglia.it/default.asp?idlingua=1&idcontenuto=25&page=Seap+Bari) (also called Bari Palese airport), which is about 70 km away. Bari Airport  is also served by low-cost  airlines such as [Ryanair](https://www.ryanair.com/gb/en/cheap-flight-destinations), [Easyjet](https://www.easyjet.com/en/routemap), Germanwings, etc (see the [airline destinations from/to Bari](https://www.bariairport.net/flights/map.shtml) ) from main European cities. If you do not find a direct flight we suggest getting a flight to Rome or Milan, spending a few days there and then continuing your journey by train or air.

There is another airport: Brindisi ([https://www.aeroportidipuglia.it/homepagebrindisi](https://www.aeroportidipuglia.it/homepagebrindisi)) that is a bit further then Bari but a good option if you find a direct flight from your country.

#### Bari Airport to Matera

- Taxi: (80-100 Euro and 1h 15 min each way), get in touch with other course participants fopr ride shares.
- Bus: Direct bus service from Bari Airport to Matera (~5 Euro), running several times a day (1h 15 min trip). The ticket can be bought at the ticket machines (advisable, as the buses fill up quickly during busy times of the day) or from the driver. Ask the airport info desk if you need help.
- Train: the airport is connected by train to Bari Central Station, and from there you have to get a train to Matera. Note that there are no trains on Sundays. Info at https://www.trenitalia.com/

#### Alternative Routes to Matera

You can fly to Rome, Naples and connect to Matera:

- There is a bus service [Marozzi](http://www.marozzivt.it/ "http://www.marozzivt.it") which connect main Italian cities with Matera.
- [SITA](http://www.sitabus.it/ "http://www.sitabus.it/") operates bus services from Matera to Taranto and Metaponto as well as other local towns and villages.
